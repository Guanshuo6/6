About the club

introduction

On September 1, 2017, this Club Co., Ltd. was officially listed on the New Third Board with stock code: 871888, becoming the first team listed in the CBA, creating a precedent for the marketization of basketball in China. In addition to participating in the CBA league, the club holds various basketball-related events and activities every offseason. And committed to participating in public welfare activities from all walks of life to better promote basketball to various fields. The club takes root in Guangfo, and with the help of the huge market capacity in this region, it further cultivates the international, professional and market-oriented club operation concept. And with the NBA American Professional Basketball League clubs and stadiums as a model, we continue to improve supporting products and services, and create a new model and development path for China's sports industry.

The club is open daily from 9.00.am to 8.00.pm
